**Part A: Add cce_vs_epi_thickness to src/charge_collection.py:**

`cce_vs_epi_thickness(epi_range_cm, V_bias=-30.0, alpha_range_cm=15e-4, generation_rate=1e18)`:

- Sweep epi thickness from 5 to 20 um (5e-4 to 20e-4 cm) at fixed reverse bias.
- For each thickness: create fresh DD device with that epi, ramp to V_bias, add alpha generation, compute CCE.
- Use unique device names with uuid; delete each after extraction.
- Expected physics: CCE decreases with epi thickness at fixed bias because thicker epi is harder to fully deplete, and some charge is generated in neutral region with incomplete collection.
- Return dict: epi_thicknesses, cce_values, V_bias.
