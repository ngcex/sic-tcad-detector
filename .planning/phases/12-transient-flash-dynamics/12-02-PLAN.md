     c. Concatenate all per-pulse time/current arrays, offsetting times so they are continuous: pulse_i_times += sum of previous pulse durations

   IMPORTANT: Also modify simulate_pulse to accept `skip_init=False` parameter. When skip_init=True, skip the zeroed-generation + transient_dc initialization (device already has transient state from previous pulse).
